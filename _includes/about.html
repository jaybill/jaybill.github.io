"Jaybill is just this guy, you know?"

This isn't a blog in any traditional sense. It's a collection of stuff I wrote, most of it short and weird. Sometimes it's microbloggy sort of stuff, sometimes it's short fiction. It's a mixed bag, really. Maybe you'll dig it. I don't know. I can't keep up with you people anymore.

"What's with the name?"

Short Ride in a Fast Machine is an orchestral composition by John Adams.

"What's with the design of this site?"

This site uses the Solarized color scheme created by Ethan Schoonover. The site is supposed to look like a text editor, which is where I spend most of my time in front of a computer anyway.

"Can I contact you?"

If you want to talk to me about something on this site, feel free to email me here. If you want to talk me about something else, please use the appropriate channels to do so.
